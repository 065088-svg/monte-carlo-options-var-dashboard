Model mechanics
GBM price path (risk-neutral)
S(t+dt) = S(t) · exp[(r − σ²/2)dt + σ√dt · Z]
Z ~ N(0,1) | antithetic variates used
Each path discretises over 252 trading steps. Antithetic variates (pairing Z with −Z) halves variance of the estimator without extra computation.
VaR & Expected Shortfall (ES)
VaR₉₉ = −Percentile(P&L, 1%)
ES₉₉ = −E[P&L | P&L ≤ −VaR₉₉]
ES (CVaR) answers "given we're in the tail, how bad is it on average?" — preferred over VaR under Basel III for its sub-additivity and tail-sensitivity.
